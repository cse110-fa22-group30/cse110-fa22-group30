# Decision on whether adding a background image or not

​

* Status: Accepted
* Deciders: John and Xiaofan <!-- optional -->
* Date: 2022-12-06 <!-- optional -->
​

## Context and Problem Statement

​
Whether to add a background image to make the page more colorful.
​

## Decision Drivers <!-- optional -->

​

* Professionalism
* The look
* Emphasis the important feature
​

## Considered Options

​

* Add a background image
* No background image
​

## Decision Outcome

​
Chosen option: No background image

## Pros and Cons of the Options <!-- optional -->

### Add a background image

​

* Good, the site will look more colorful
* Good, it can attract people's eyes
* Bad, reduce the professionalism of the site
* Bad, some people may like it simple
* Bad, people may not like the design
​

### No background image

​

* Good, the site can look more simple
* Good, the feature of our site can be more emphasized
* Good, it can be easier for the user to recieve information from a simple page
* Bad, the site may look very boring
